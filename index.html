Career Timeline

Key milestones in Babar Azam's cricket journey

2015

International Debut

Made ODI debut against Zimbabwe in Lahore. Scored a half-century in his second match.

2016

Maiden Century

Scored his first ODI century (120) against West Indies in Sharjah.

2019

ICC World Cup

Scored 474 runs in World Cup 2019, becoming Pakistan's highest run-scorer in the tournament.

2020

Captaincy

Appointed captain of Pakistan's ODI and T20I teams.

2021

Test Captain

Appointed Test captain of Pakistan, becoming captain in all formats.

2022

ICC Awards

Won ICC ODI Player of the Year and ICC Men's Cricketer of the Year awards.

Records & Achievements

Notable records held by Babar Azam

Fastest to 2000 ODI Runs

Reached 2000 ODI runs in just 45 innings, breaking Virat Kohli's record.

ICC No.1 Ranking

Became the first Pakistani batsman to achieve No.1 ranking in ICC ODI rankings.

Most Consecutive 50+ Scores

Scored 50+ runs in 9 consecutive innings across formats (2021-2022).

ICC Awards 2022

First Pakistani to win ICC ODI Player of the Year and ICC Men's Cricketer of the Year.
